⚠️ 💊 Medications

Prepare a list of medications and be ready to discuss them with the nurse or scheduler when they call.

Stop anti-diarrhea medicines (Imodium, Kaopectate) and fiber supplements (Metamucil) 7 days before.

Stop GLP-1 medications (Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Trulicity) 7 days before — these slow stomach emptying and increase anesthesia risk.

Give routine morning medicines with a small sip of water or breast milk unless told otherwise.

📅 1 Day Before the Procedure

If your child eats solids, follow a low-residue (“white”) diet through lunch — then clear liquids only after 2:00 PM. If your child takes only breast milk or formula, continue feeding normally until the fasting times below.
